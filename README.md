# 🚗 CitySpot

### IoT-Enabled Smart Parking Platform with Real-Time Monitoring

CitySpot is a smart parking platform designed to make parking simpler, faster, and more efficient for everyone.

Whether it's a residential society, row house, shopping mall, office complex, or public parking area, CitySpot helps drivers find available parking spaces while allowing parking owners to manage and utilize their unused parking spots more effectively.

The platform focuses on real-time parking visibility, seamless booking, and future-ready IoT integration to create a smarter parking experience.

---

## 🌟 Why CitySpot?

Finding a parking space in busy areas can be frustrating and time-consuming. At the same time, many parking spaces remain unused for large parts of the day.

CitySpot aims to solve this problem by connecting:

* Drivers looking for parking
* Parking space owners with available slots
* Administrators managing the overall system

The goal is simple:

> Make parking easier to find, easier to manage, and smarter to monitor.

---

## 🎯 What CitySpot Offers

### For Drivers

* Search nearby parking spaces

### For Parking Owners

### For Administrators

* Manage users and parking listings

## ⚡ Key Features

### Real-Time Parking Availability

Users can instantly view the current status of parking spaces, helping them make quicker and smarter decisions.

### Smart Parking Discovery

Find nearby parking spaces through map-based and list-based views with filtering options.

### Automated Booking System

Booking is handled automatically by the system without requiring manual approval from parking owners.

### Role-Based Experience

A single account can switch between:

* Driver Mode
* Parking Owner Mode

This allows users to both rent out parking spaces and reserve parking when needed.

### Future IoT Integration

CitySpot is being designed with future IoT support in mind, enabling real-time parking monitoring through smart devices and sensors.
